Synesthesia

Synesthesia is a blending senses condition characterised by sensory pairings. Synesthetes are people who can see sound, taste words and smell colours...they experience additional sensation in secondary modality. According to Dr. Sean A. Day,"there are at least 80 types of synesthesia, likely more". www.daysyn.com.
