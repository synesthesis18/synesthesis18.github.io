Synesthesia

Synesthesia is a blending senses condition characterised by sensory pairings. Synesthetes are people who can see sound, taste words and smell colours...they experience additional sensation in secondary modality. According to Dr. Sean A. Day,"there are at least 80 types of synesthesia, likely more". daysyn.com

Consciousness

Anil Seth, Professor of Cognitive and Computational Neuroscience, with his team, conducted an experiment to train non-synaesthetes for colour-grapheme synaesthesia, and stated that after 9 weeks the participants passed the test:"results of an IQ improvement therefore provisionally indicate that cognitive training including synaesthetic associations may in the future be a promising new tool for vulnerable clinical groups to enhance general mental ability". sussex.ac.uk/sackler
